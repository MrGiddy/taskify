What is Taskify?

Taskify is a simple task management application designed to help you organize your tasks and boost your productivity. Whether you're managing personal projects or collaborating with a team, Taskify has the tools you need to stay on top of your tasks.

Features

  • Create and manage tasks easily
  • Set due dates and priorities
  • Update task status and details
  • Persistent storage
  • Receive notifications and reminders
